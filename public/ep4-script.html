Prediction Model Updates

NOTE: Episode 3 explained HOW the model works. This section is about what CHANGED and the RESULTS. Don't re-explain Poisson or the 12 factors.
"Three upgrades to the prediction engine since last episode.

First - all those match stats now actually feed into predictions. xG, shots on target, possession - real underlying data instead of just looking at final scores.

Second - player form matters now. Individual player goals, assists, ratings directly influence match predictions. Injuries measurably weaken a team's predicted output.

Third - the self-learning loop was broken. Found the bug. It was treating every factor the same regardless of how they performed. Now each factor is individually scored. The model gets smarter after every result."

The Proof

"We back-tested against 4,191 completed fixtures. The real numbers:"
Overall accuracy: 50.6% (random = 33%)
HIGH confidence picks: 59.2%
Over/Under 2.5: 58% (was 45%)
Both Teams to Score: 55% (was 45%)
Exact scores: 10.2%
"The headline: when the model is confident, it's right 6 times out of 10. The O/U and BTTS markets went from worse than a coin flip to genuinely useful after we recalibrated.

Every prediction now comes with full betting lines. Over/under, both teams to score, top scorelines, expected goals. It's a proper sports analytics product."
